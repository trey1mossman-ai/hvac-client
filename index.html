Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start my flooring project? +
We typically schedule installations within 1-2 weeks of approval. For emergency situations like water damage, we offer 24/7 response. Our Chicago crews are strategically located to serve all neighborhoods quickly.
Do you offer financing for flooring projects? +
Yes, we work with several financing partners to make your flooring project affordable. We offer 0% interest options for qualified buyers and can discuss payment plans during your free estimate.
What areas of Chicago do you serve? +
We serve all of Chicago and surrounding suburbs including Evanston, Oak Park, Naperville, Schaumburg, and more. Our crews cover a 50-mile radius from downtown Chicago.
How long does flooring installation typically take? +
Most residential projects complete in 1-3 days. A typical 1,000 sq ft installation takes 1-2 days depending on the material and prep work needed. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily life.
Do you move furniture during installation? +
Yes, we handle furniture moving as part of our service. Our experienced crews use proper equipment to protect your belongings. We recommend removing small valuables and breakables before we arrive.
What warranty do you offer on installations? +
We provide a comprehensive warranty on all installations, in addition to manufacturer warranties on materials. Our workmanship is guaranteed, and we stand behind every job. Specific warranty terms vary by project type.
